Recipe

Recipe generation service for different nanofiber production technologies

  • PVDF

    An exceptional polymer for various application areas, including battery separators, filtration media, membranes, and more

  • PA6

    High production rates with ultrafine nanofiber diameters, primarily for air filtration applications

  • PAN

    High thermal and mechanical stability polymer with low density

  • PBT

    Melt-processed, chemically and dimensionally stable polymer with good UV resistance

  • PP

    Low cost, chemically inert, naturally hydrophobic polymer for melt nanofiber spinning

  • PUR

    High performance stretchable polymer mainly used for perfo apparel textile membranes

  • Chitosan

    Biocompatible and biodegradable polymer for cosmetic mask and wound dressing application

  • Collagen

    Specialty polymer for anti-aging cosmetic masks and regenerative medicine applications

  • Cellulose Acetate

    Specially developed for nanofiber based syringe filter applications

Overview

The largest, highest performing fleet of quantum systems in the world

IBM's core values include a commitment to trust and personal responsibility and a pursuit of innovation that matters to our company and the world. Consistent with these principles, IBM is committed to leading on public policy issues that are relevant to IBM and the world. IBM Government and Regulatory Affairs (GRA) provides worldwide leadership and expertise in advocating and advancing the public policy interests of IBM, its shareholders and employees with governments.
