Engineering

    Piping and instrumentation diagram generation for different processes

Precision Machining

Precision machining services for demanding tolerances and complex parts

  • 5 Axis Milling

    State-of-the-art 5-axis simultaneous and 3+2 index milling services for the most sophisticated components

  • C-Y Axis Turning

    C and Y axis turning with optional broaching and gear hobbing capability

  • Manual Turning

    Manual turning service for medium-precision rollers and cylindrical parts with a length of up to 3800 mm

  • OD Grinding

    Outer diameter grinding for cylindrical parts and technical rollers up to 780mm diameter and 4000mm length

  • Surface Grinding

    Numerical controlled surface grinding up to 800mm length and 500mm width

  • Plastic Welding

    Plastic welding service for PE, PP, PVC and PETG with rod and extruder systems

    Distinct control over air temperature and humidity, ensuring stable output

  • Mixing Units

    Properly mixing the recipe ingredients in a controlled environment

  • Effluent Treatment

    Emissions from the nanofiber process must be treated to reduce air pollution

  • Thermo Calendering

    Intended for use in thermal lamination and nanofiber membrane production

  • Lamination

    Process for manufacturing multilayered nanofiber products like HVAC media

  • Adhesion Promotion

    Essential supplementary process for enhancing the performance of surface filter media and separators

Recipe

Recipe generation service for different nanofiber production technologies

  • PVDF

    An exceptional polymer for various application areas, including battery separators, filtration media, membranes, and more

  • PA6

    High production rates with ultrafine nanofiber diameters, primarily for air filtration applications

  • PAN

    High thermal and mechanical stability polymer with low density

  • PBT

    Melt-processed, chemically and dimensionally stable polymer with good UV resistance

  • PP

    Low cost, chemically inert, naturally hydrophobic polymer for melt nanofiber spinning

  • PUR

    High performance stretchable polymer mainly used for perfo apparel textile membranes

  • Chitosan

    Biocompatible and biodegradable polymer for cosmetic mask and wound dressing application

  • Collagen

    Specialty polymer for anti-aging cosmetic masks and regenerative medicine applications

  • Cellulose Acetate

    Specially developed for nanofiber based syringe filter applications
